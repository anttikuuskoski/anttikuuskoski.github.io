This is What Makes me tick

I'm a startup kind of a guy, both by design and by experience. Here's my top three:

new value generation

I've founded, built and failed two companies during the last decade and had a marvelous time doing it.  I've seen how to move people with content and seen what digital can do, but also that successful new business is rarely about technology. I believe that true customer value is not only the requirement for success, but also the key driver in most day to day operations - or at least it should be!

Tech-product-marketing

I'm a decent developer / achitect and an expressive personality. I like communicating (if there's value to be shared) and have grown to understand how good management + culture helps a company flourish. However, I'm too impatient to become a true expert in any particular area. I believe to be able to contribute most value in the overlapping, value driven areas of technology, product and marketing.

The big picture

I've often dived deep into the details and found home there. Consequently, I've failed to understand the whys and hows of a system and stakeholders. Implementation contains too many (important and delightful) details for me to keep the big picture. And that's where my real strength is; in being able to devise and understand complex systems and strategies.

What can I do for you?

I am fluent in Customer and Business, while Tech is my second native tongue. I enjoy backends, doing things in the cloud, making teams better and hope to find long term mutually beneficial relationships in technology endeavors.

Here are some positions you'd like to consider me for: CTO (Chief Technical Officer), PO (Product Owner); however, I do hope to get my hands dirty part of the time. In many cases, a Team Leader, Lead Developer, Software Architect or similar tech-centric position would also do as well.

I'd really love to hear what it is that you do - and particularly: why. If the latter proves to be tricky, I can help you figure it out. If you already know why and are passionate about it, let's find out how to change that into brilliant products, inspired tech teams, value-filled marketing and a clear strategy!

Hi, I'm Antti!

I want to Create Stuff Worth Creating.

I enjoy doing most things where I can truly apply my energy, creativity and perseverance. Yesterday is a great source of learning, but tomorrow is where my passions lay - I want to be creating.
